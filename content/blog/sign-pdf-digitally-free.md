## Are Digital Signatures Legally Valid?

In most jurisdictions, yes:

- **United States** — The ESIGN Act (2000) and UETA make electronic signatures legally equivalent to handwritten signatures for most business documents.
- **European Union** — The eIDAS regulation recognizes electronic signatures. Simple electronic signatures (what this tool creates) are valid for most everyday documents.
- **India** — The Information Technology Act recognizes electronic signatures. For most personal and business documents (contracts, authorization letters, consent forms), a simple electronic signature is accepted.
- **United Kingdom, Canada, Australia** — All have laws recognizing electronic signatures for standard business use.

**Where you still need a wet signature or certified digital signature:**
- Real estate transactions (some jurisdictions)
- Wills and testaments
- Court-filed documents
- Certain government forms that explicitly require notarization

For everyday business documents, an electronically signed PDF is fully valid.

## Tips for a Professional Signature

**Practice first.** Drawing with a mouse feels different from a pen. Try a few times to get a signature you're happy with. On a phone or tablet, your finger or stylus gives more natural control.

**Use a consistent signature.** For business documents, consistency matters. Some people save their signature as an image and upload it each time for a uniform look.

**Size matters.** Don't make the signature too large (looks aggressive) or too tiny (hard to verify). Match the size of the signature line on the document.

**Position precisely.** Place the signature directly on the signature line. A floating signature in the middle of the page looks unprofessional.

**Add the date.** Many documents require a date alongside the signature. Add it using the text tool.

## FAQ

**Can I add multiple signatures to one PDF?**
Yes. Add your signature on the main signature page, and add initials or additional signatures on other pages as needed.

**Can I sign on behalf of someone else?**
Only if you have legal authority (power of attorney, authorized signatory). The tool doesn't verify identity — it places a signature where you tell it to.

**Does signing change the PDF quality?**
No. The signature is added as an overlay. The original document content stays unchanged.

**Can I remove a signature after signing?**
Not from the downloaded PDF — the signature is embedded. To start over, upload the original unsigned PDF and sign again.

**Is this the same as Adobe Sign or DocuSign?**
Those services add identity verification, audit trails, and multi-party signing workflows. This tool handles the simple case: you have a PDF, you need to sign it, and you want it done in 30 seconds.

**What about signing PDFs with an Aadhaar-based digital signature?**
Aadhaar eSign (via DSC) is a certified digital signature recognized by the Indian government for official filings. This tool creates simple electronic signatures, which are valid for most everyday documents but not for filings that require Aadhaar DSC.
